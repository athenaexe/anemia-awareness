Anemia, a preventable condition where your blood lacks healthy red blood cells, causes fatigue, weakness, and shortness of breath by limiting oxygen delivery to your tissues. Making informed, blood-healthy choices can help you prevent this common issue and improve your energy and focus.

Strategies & How to Overcome Barriers

Iron Intake

Choose simple iron-rich foods: lentils, beans, tofu, spinach, fortified cereals, and lean meats/poultry.

Vitamin C Boost

Add a Vitamin C source to iron-rich meals (e.g., bell peppers, oranges, tomatoes) to triple absorption.

B12 Check

For those limiting animal products: include B12-fortified foods or supplements.

Consultation

Don't self-diagnose! See a doctor if you have persistent symptoms like fatigue, pale skin, or weakness.
